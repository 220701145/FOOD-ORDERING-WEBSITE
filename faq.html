Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our menu, select the items you want, and click "Add to Cart." Once you're ready, proceed to checkout, enter your delivery details, and confirm your order. You can pay via credit/debit card, net banking, or choose cash on delivery.

2. What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver across all major areas in the city. To check if we deliver to your area, simply enter your address during checkout or check our delivery zones page.

3. What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary based on your location and the restaurant you're ordering from. However, we often have promotions that include free delivery for certain orders. Check the promotions section for ongoing offers.

4. How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location and the time of day. Typically, deliveries take 30-45 minutes. You will receive an estimated delivery time when you place your order.

5. What if my order is late?

If your order is running late, don't worry! You can track your delivery in real-time through our app. In case of significant delays, you can also reach out to our customer support team for assistance.

6. Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled within a few minutes of placing them, as long as the restaurant has not started preparing the food. To cancel, go to your order history and click "Cancel Order."

7. What if I receive the wrong order?

If you receive an incorrect order, please contact our support team immediately, and we will arrange for a replacement or refund.

8. How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via the "Help" section in the app or call our helpline number for immediate assistance.
